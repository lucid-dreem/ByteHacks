Since 1941, Jews from all across Europe have been gathered and forced into
concentration camps where they faced the harshest forms of torture. Adolf Hitler,
the German Nazi's dictator, used the Jews as a scapegoat as a reason for many faults.
In concentration camps, the Jews were forced into rough labor, gas chambers, and
experimented upon, just to name a few.
