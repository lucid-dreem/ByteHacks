Camps for Displaced Persons




As Jews and other people from concentration camps were liberated by Allied armies towards the end of World War II, many had no place to return to as their homes had been destroyed, or due to the Anti-Semitic environments of their cities and towns. Many fled to designated camps for such displaced persons set up by the Allies. Below is compiled a non-exhaustive list of camps that provided shelter to these people. Citizens at the time would potentially have been able to add camps to the list as observed in person, from the news, or from other verified sources.
